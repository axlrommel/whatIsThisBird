Instructions:

1. Click on 'Import' to upload your picture

2. Use the hand tool to center the image, and the slider on the right to zoom in and out

3. Select the part of the picture with the most representative colors, but leave some background as the image filter expects it

4. Click 'Find!' and wait about 10 seconds to get your results
